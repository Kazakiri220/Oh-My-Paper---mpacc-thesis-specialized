# Defense Prep

Generate defense materials only from the confirmed topic, outline, data, and draft. Do not invent results.

## Outputs

- 5-8 minute self-statement.
- PPT outline.
- Likely questions and answer points.
- Weakness/limitation response plan.
- Data and citation verification checklist.

## Question Types

- Why choose this topic and case?
- What is the theoretical basis, and how is it used?
- Why choose this method/framework?
- What data supports the key finding?
- What is the innovation?
- What are the limitations?
- Are recommendations feasible for the case organization?
- How did you ensure citation and data authenticity?

## Answer Pattern

`Direct answer -> evidence/method -> relation to thesis contribution -> limitation if any`.

Good: "The case is representative because it combines public disclosure, operational process material, and a current accounting-policy problem; the limitation is that internal interviews cover only finance and operations departments."

Weak: "This topic is important and innovative, so I chose it."
