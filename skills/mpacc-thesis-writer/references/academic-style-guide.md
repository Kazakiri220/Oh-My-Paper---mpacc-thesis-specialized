# Academic Style Guide

Write in a restrained, evidence-led Chinese academic style. The goal is clarity and verifiability, not ornate phrasing.

## Sentence Rules

- Prefer concrete subjects: `A 公司预算编制流程` instead of `相关工作`.
- Prefer evidence verbs: `显示`, `表明`, `反映`, `导致`, `约束`.
- Keep one paragraph to one analytical point.
- Use "evidence -> interpretation -> implication" order.
- Avoid opening every paragraph with `首先、其次、最后`.

## Reduce Generic Or AI-Like Prose

Flag and revise phrases such as:

- `具有重要意义` without explaining for whom and why;
- `至关重要`, `不言而喻`, `值得注意的是`;
- `多措并举`, `全面提升`, `进一步加强` without concrete action;
- summary sentences that merely repeat the heading.

Good: "2019-2023 年，A 公司销售费用率持续高于行业中位数，说明其渠道投入尚未转化为相应收入增长。"

Weak: "A 公司应全面加强成本管理，这对企业高质量发展具有至关重要的意义。"

## Tables And Figures

- Use tables for literature matrix, indicator systems, evidence inventories, problem-cause-solution mapping, and data comparison.
- Use figures for technical routes, process redesign, risk transmission paths, model architecture, and resource orchestration stages.
- Every table/figure needs title, source note, and interpretation in the text.

## Academic Tone

- Do not overclaim causality when the method only supports association or case inference.
- Separate fact, inference, and recommendation.
- Mark limitations honestly.
